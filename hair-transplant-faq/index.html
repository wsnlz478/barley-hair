Hair Transplant FAQ

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about hair transplant procedures, recovery, costs, and more.

Procedure Questions

Is hair transplant surgery painful? +

Hair transplant surgery is generally well-tolerated by most patients. We use local anesthesia to numb the scalp during the procedure, so you shouldn't feel pain during the surgery. Some patients may experience mild discomfort or soreness in the first few days after the procedure, which can be easily managed with prescribed pain medication.

How long does a hair transplant procedure take? +

The duration of a hair transplant procedure depends on the number of grafts being transplanted. A typical FUE procedure with 1,500-2,500 grafts takes about 4-6 hours. Larger procedures with 3,000+ grafts may take 6-8 hours or may be split into two sessions. Our microneedle technique allows for efficient transplantation while maintaining high quality.

What is the difference between FUE and FUT? +

FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) involves extracting individual hair follicles directly from the donor area, leaving tiny, virtually undetectable scars. FUT (Follicular Unit Transplantation) involves removing a strip of scalp from the donor area and then suturing it closed, leaving a linear scar. FUE is generally preferred for its minimal scarring and faster recovery. At Barley, we specialize in FUE with advanced microneedle implantation technology.

What is microneedle hair transplant? +

Microneedle hair transplant is an advanced technique that uses an implanter pen device to precisely place hair follicles into the recipient area. This method allows for better control over the angle, depth, and direction of each implanted follicle, resulting in more natural-looking results. Barley introduced this technology in China and holds multiple national patents for microneedle hair transplant techniques.

Can women get hair transplants? +

Yes! Hair transplant surgery is suitable for both men and women experiencing hair loss. While male pattern baldness is more commonly discussed, women can also benefit from hair restoration procedures. The approach may differ slightly based on the type of hair loss, but our experienced surgeons can create personalized treatment plans for women as well.

Recovery Questions

How long is the recovery period after a hair transplant? +

Most patients can return to normal daily activities within 2-3 days after an FUE hair transplant. The initial redness and scabbing typically subside within 7-10 days. You'll need to avoid strenuous exercise and direct sun exposure for about 2 weeks. The transplanted hair will enter a resting phase and may shed temporarily around 2-4 weeks after the procedure, which is normal.

When will I see results from my hair transplant? +

You'll start to see new hair growth around 3-4 months after the procedure. The hair will continue to grow and thicken over the following months. Most patients see significant results by 6-9 months, with final results visible at 12-18 months. The growth process is gradual, and each person's timeline may vary slightly.

What should I do after my hair transplant? +

After your hair transplant, you should: keep your head elevated while sleeping for the first few days, avoid touching or scratching the transplanted area, follow the washing instructions provided by your surgeon, avoid strenuous exercise for 2 weeks, protect your scalp from direct sun exposure, and attend all follow-up appointments. We'll provide you with detailed post-operative care instructions.

Are there any side effects? +

Side effects from hair transplant surgery are typically mild and temporary. These may include: mild swelling, redness, scabbing, itching, and temporary numbness or sensitivity in the treated areas. These symptoms usually resolve within a few days to a couple of weeks. Serious complications are rare when the procedure is performed by experienced surgeons like our team at Barley.

Results Questions

Will hair transplant results look natural? +

Yes! When performed by experienced surgeons using advanced techniques like our microneedle method, hair transplant results can look highly natural. Our surgeons carefully design the hairline and place each follicle at the correct angle and direction to match your natural hair growth pattern. The result is hair that looks and feels very natural.

How long do hair transplant results last? +

Hair transplant results are long-lasting. The hair follicles used in the transplant are taken from the donor area (typically the back and sides of the head), which contains hair that is genetically resistant to balding. Once these follicles are transplanted, they continue to grow naturally for many years. While you may continue to experience some natural hair loss as you age, the transplanted hair will remain.

How many sessions will I need? +

The number of sessions needed depends on your goals and the extent of your hair loss. Many patients achieve their desired results with a single session, especially with our advanced microneedle technique that allows for high-density transplantation. For more advanced baldness or if you want maximum density, a second session may be recommended 6-12 months after the first.

What if I don't see good results? +

At Barley, we have an excellent track record of successful hair transplant procedures with high patient satisfaction rates. Our experienced surgeons use the latest technology and techniques to ensure optimal results. We also provide comprehensive follow-up care to monitor your progress. In the rare event that results aren't as expected, we'll work with you to determine the most suitable course of action.
