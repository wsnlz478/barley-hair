What is Scar Hair Transplant?

Scar hair transplant is a specialized procedure designed to restore hair growth on areas of the scalp that have been damaged by trauma, surgery, burns, or other injuries. Scar tissue typically lacks hair follicles, making it impossible for hair to grow naturally in these areas.

At Barley, our expert surgeons use advanced microneedle technology to carefully transplant healthy hair follicles from donor areas into scarred tissue. With over 20 years of experience and 200,000+ successful procedures, we have the expertise to deliver outstanding results even in challenging scar restoration cases.

  • ✅ Suitable for all types of scars
  • ✅ Natural-looking hair growth direction
  • ✅ Minimal additional scarring
  • ✅ High graft survival rate
  • ✅ Permanent, long-lasting results
