Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from international patients considering Turkey

Do I need a visa to visit Turkey?

Many nationalities can obtain an e-visa online before travel or receive visa-free entry for tourism. We recommend checking Turkey's official e-visa website for your specific nationality. Our team can provide invitation letters if needed for your visa application.

How much does a hair transplant cost in Turkey?

Costs typically range from 1,500 to 4,000 EUR depending on the number of grafts and procedure type. We offer all-inclusive packages that may include accommodation, transfers, and the procedure. Contact us for a personalized quote.

How long should I stay in Turkey for the procedure?

We recommend staying 5-7 days: 1 day for consultation, 1 day for the procedure, and 3-5 days for follow-up and initial recovery. This allows adequate time for the first wash and check-up before traveling home.

Is Turkey safe for medical tourism?

Yes, Turkey is one of the world's leading destinations for medical tourism, hosting over 1 million medical tourists annually. Istanbul's hospitals meet international accreditation standards. Our facility follows strict safety and hygiene protocols.

What language do the staff speak?

Our Turkey team includes English-speaking patient coordinators, surgeons, and nursing staff. We also offer support in Arabic, Russian, and other languages upon request. All consultations and procedures are conducted in your preferred language.
