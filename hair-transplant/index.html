Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about hair transplant at Barley

💰 How much does a hair transplant cost?

Hair transplant costs vary based on the number of grafts needed and the extent of hair loss. At Barley, prices typically range from $3,000 to $15,000 USD, with an average cost of $5,000-$8,000 for most patients. We offer free consultations to provide personalized quotes.

⏱️ How long does recovery take?

Most patients return to normal activities within 3-5 days. Initial healing takes 7-10 days, with transplanted hair shedding after 2-4 weeks. New growth begins at 3-4 months, with full results visible at 12-18 months post-procedure.

♾️ Are results long-lasting?

Yes, hair transplant results are long-lasting. The transplanted hair follicles are taken from the donor area (back and sides of the head) which is resistant to DHT, the hormone that causes hair loss. Once transplanted, these follicles will continue to grow naturally for life.

🔢 How many grafts do I need?

The number of grafts needed depends on the extent of hair loss and desired density. Typical procedures range from 1,500 to 4,000 grafts. A Norwood Class 3-4 baldness usually requires 2,000-3,000 grafts, while more extensive baldness may need 3,500-5,000 grafts.

😌 Is the procedure painful?

The procedure is performed under local anesthesia, so you should not feel pain during surgery. Most patients describe the experience as comfortable, often watching movies or resting during the 6-8 hour procedure. Post-operative discomfort is minimal and manageable with prescribed pain medication.

📅 When can I see final results?

Hair growth follows a natural timeline: shedding at 2-4 weeks, new growth starts at 3-4 months, significant improvement at 6 months, and full results at 12-18 months. The hair continues to thicken and mature over time, looking increasingly natural.
