What is FUE Hair Transplant?

Advanced follicular unit extraction for seamless, natural-looking hair restoration

🔬

Individual Follicle Extraction

FUE harvests individual hair follicles directly from the donor area rather than removing a hair strip, resulting in minimal to no surgical scar and hair restoration that appears seamless and natural.

⏱️

Minimal Downtime

With tiny dot-like incisions instead of a linear scar, FUE patients experience faster healing and can return to work in just 3-7 days. Suitable for all hair types and textures.

✨

Natural Hair Growth

Individual follicle units are extracted and placed in the recipient area for permanent hair regeneration. Natural hair growth patterns ensure results that look completely authentic.

Step-by-step process for natural-looking hair restoration

Preparation & Anesthesia

Strategic Implantation

About FUE Hair Transplant

One of the most popular and effective hair restoration methods available today

Our FUE differs from traditional FUE in that we combine it with our microneedle technology to maximize the number of grafts. This is beneficial when a client has thin or sparse hair in the donor area.

At Barley, all procedures are planned and carried out under the supervision of an experienced doctor, who first consults with the clients to determine the best course of action for the FUE follicle extraction surgery.
