Why Choose Our Eyebrow Transplant?

Advanced microneedle technology for natural, ideal eyebrows

Barley's microneedle eyebrow transplant technique uses an implanter pen to precisely place each hair follicle at the ideal angle and depth. This advanced method allows our surgeons to create a natural-looking arch with the ideal hair direction and density.

The procedure is performed under local anesthesia and typically takes 2-3 hours. Since the hair is taken from your own scalp, the results are completely natural and long-lasting. The transplanted hair will grow just like your natural eyebrow hair and can be trimmed and styled as desired.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about eyebrow transplant at Barley

💰 How much does an eyebrow transplant cost?

Eyebrow transplant costs at Barley typically range from $2,000 to $5,000 USD, depending on the density and shape desired. A complete eyebrow restoration usually requires 300-600 grafts per eyebrow. We offer free consultations to provide accurate quotes.

⏱️ How long does recovery take?

Eyebrow transplant recovery is quick. Most patients return to normal activities within 2-3 days. Initial healing takes 5-7 days, with transplanted hair shedding after 2-3 weeks. New eyebrow growth begins at 3-4 months, with full results visible at 6-9 months.

✨ Will eyebrow hair grow like natural eyebrows?

Yes, transplanted eyebrow hair grows naturally. However, because the hair is taken from the scalp, it will grow longer than natural eyebrow hair and will need regular trimming. Our surgeons carefully control the angle and direction to match natural eyebrow growth patterns.

🎨 Can I choose the shape of my new eyebrows?

Absolutely! During your consultation, we'll design the ideal eyebrow shape based on your facial features, bone structure, and personal preferences. We can create anything from a subtle arch to a dramatic curve, with precise control over thickness and length.

🤔 Is eyebrow transplant better than microblading?

Eyebrow transplant offers long-lasting, natural hair growth, while microblading is a semi-permanent makeup technique that lasts 1-2 years. Transplants provide real hair that grows naturally, can be styled, and doesn't require touch-ups. However, microblading is less invasive and more affordable initially.

📅 When can I see final results?

Eyebrow hair growth follows a natural timeline: shedding at 2-3 weeks, new growth starts at 3-4 months, significant improvement at 6 months, and full results at 6-9 months. The eyebrows continue to mature and look increasingly natural over time.
