Understanding Hair Transplant Results

What you need to know about your hair transplant journey and expected outcomes.

📅

Week 1-2

Initial healing phase. Transplanted hair may shed - this is completely normal and part of the process.

🌱

Month 2-4

New hair growth begins. Thin, fine hairs start emerging from transplanted follicles.

💪

Month 5-8

Hair thickens and becomes more visible. Noticeable improvement in density and coverage.

✨

Month 9-12

Final results emerge. Full, natural-looking hair with 95%+ graft survival rate achieved.
