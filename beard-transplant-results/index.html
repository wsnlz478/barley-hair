Understanding Your Beard Transplant Journey

From consultation to your final results, here's what you can expect at every stage of your beard restoration.

📋

Consultation

Our experts assess your facial hair goals, donor area quality, and design a personalized beard shape.

🏥

Procedure Day

4-6 hour procedure under local anesthesia. Each graft is placed at the precise angle for natural beard growth.

🌱

Recovery (Week 1-4)

Minimal downtime. Small scabs form and fall off within 7-10 days. Most patients return to work within 2 days.

🧔

Final Results (6-12 Months)

Your new beard grows naturally. You can shave, trim, and style it just like your original facial hair.
