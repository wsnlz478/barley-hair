Factors That Affect Hair Transplant Cost

Every hair transplant is unique because every patient's situation is different

Number of Grafts Required

The most significant factor. A graft contains 1-4 hair follicles. More extensive hair loss requires more grafts for optimal density and coverage. Mild loss: 500-1,000 grafts. Moderate: 1,000-2,000. Advanced: 2,000-4,000+.

Technique Used

Different techniques have different costs. FUT is cost-effective for larger procedures. FUE offers no linear scar with slightly higher per-graft cost. Microneedle transplant provides excellent results with patented technology.

Area(s) Being Treated

The specific areas affect both graft count and complexity: hairline restoration, crown/vertex, mid-scalp coverage, full scalp restoration, or specialized areas like eyebrows, beard, and sideburns.

Surgeon Experience & Technology

Highly experienced surgeons with specialized training deliver excellent results with higher graft survival rates. Modern facilities with advanced technology ensure better outcomes and patient safety.
