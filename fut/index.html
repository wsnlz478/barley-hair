What is FUT Hair Transplant?

The proven strip harvesting method for maximum graft yield and natural results

🔬

Strip Harvesting

FUT involves surgically removing a thin strip of tissue from the donor area, which is then dissected into individual follicular units under a microscope for implantation.

🎯

Maximum Graft Yield

FUT allows harvesting the largest number of grafts in a single session, making it ideal for patients with advanced hair loss who need extensive coverage.

✨

Permanent Results

Transplanted hair grows naturally and permanently. The linear scar is barely visible when healed properly, hidden by surrounding hair.

About FUT Hair Transplant

The gold standard for maximum graft yield in a single session

Our FUT differs from traditional FUT in that we combine it with our microneedle technology to maximize the number of grafts. This is beneficial when a client has thin or sparse hair in the donor area.

As a result, we only use common FUT on rare occasions, and only when our clients have thick and wavy hair that is suitable for FUT. The combination of FUT and our microneedle methods has produced excellent results for our clients.

In our practice at Barley, all procedures are planned and carried out under the supervision of an experienced Barley doctor, who first consults with the clients to determine the best course of action.
