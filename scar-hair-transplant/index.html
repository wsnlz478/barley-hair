Why Choose Our Scar Treatment?

Advanced microneedle technology for effective scar camouflage

Scar tissue presents unique challenges for hair transplantation due to reduced blood supply and altered tissue structure. Barley's advanced microneedle technique is particularly effective for scar treatment because the implanter pen allows for precise, gentle placement of follicles that minimizes trauma to the scar tissue.

Before your procedure, our experienced surgeons will carefully evaluate your scar to assess blood supply, tissue quality, and the most suitable approach for optimal results. We may recommend a multi-stage approach for larger or more complex scars to help achieve a favorable outcome.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about scar hair transplant at Barley

💰 How much does scar hair transplant cost?

Scar hair transplant costs at Barley typically range from $2,000 to $8,000 USD, depending on the size and type of scar. Smaller scars may require 500-1,000 grafts, while larger scars may need 1,500-3,000 grafts. We offer free consultations to assess your scar and provide an accurate quote.

🔬 Can hair be transplanted into scar tissue?

Yes, hair can be transplanted into scar tissue, but it requires specialized techniques. Scar tissue has reduced blood supply, so graft survival rates may be slightly lower than in normal skin. Our microneedle technology ensures optimal graft placement and higher survival rates in scar tissue.

⏱️ How long does scar transplant recovery take?

Recovery is similar to regular hair transplant. Most patients return to normal activities within 3-5 days. Initial healing takes 7-10 days. Transplanted hair sheds after 2-4 weeks, with new growth starting at 3-4 months. Full results are visible at 12-18 months.

🩹 What types of scars can be treated?

We can treat various types of scars: FUT strip scars, FUE extraction scars, injury scars, burn scars, and surgical scars. The success rate depends on scar maturity (older scars respond better), blood supply, and scar thickness. During consultation, we'll assess whether your scar is suitable for transplant.

✨ Will the scar completely disappear after transplant?

While hair transplant can significantly camouflage scars, complete disappearance depends on scar type and size. Most scars become 70-90% less visible after transplant. The transplanted hair grows naturally over the scar, effectively hiding it. However, very thick or raised scars may not respond as well.

📅 When can I see final results?

Hair growth follows a natural timeline: shedding at 2-4 weeks, new growth starts at 3-4 months, significant improvement at 6 months, and full results at 12-18 months. The hair continues to thicken and mature over time, effectively camouflaging the scar.
