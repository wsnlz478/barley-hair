Frequently Asked Questions About Choosing Barley

Common questions from patients considering Barley for their hair restoration

How is Barley different from other hair transplant hospitals?

Barley introduced microneedle hair transplant technology in China in 2006 and holds 14 national patents. With 31 branches, 100+ experienced doctors, and over 200,000 successful procedures, we combine advanced technology with extensive clinical experience. Our patented implanter pen achieves 95%+ graft survival rates and highly natural-looking results in the industry.

What makes Barley's microneedle technology excellent?

Our patented microneedle implanter pen uses an ultra-fine 0.6-0.8mm needle that performs channel creation and follicle implantation in a single step. This provides 360-degree control over angle, direction, and depth, resulting in more natural hairlines, higher density, faster recovery (3-5 days), and 95%+ graft survival rates compared to 85-90% with traditional methods.

Do you treat international patients?

Yes! We welcome patients from around the world. Our English-speaking medical staff ensures clear communication throughout your journey. We provide complimentary airport pickup and drop-off, assistance with visa arrangements, hotel accommodations, and comprehensive follow-up care. Many international patients choose Barley for our combination of world-class expertise and excellent value.

What is the success rate of Barley hair transplants?

Barley maintains a 95%+ graft survival rate, significantly higher than the industry average of 85-90%. With over 200,000 successful procedures and 20+ years of clinical experience, our track record speaks for itself. We also offer comprehensive post-operative care to ensure optimal results for every patient.

How do I get started with Barley?

Getting started is easy. Schedule your free consultation by phone, email, WeChat, or WhatsApp. During the consultation, our experts will assess your hair loss, discuss your goals, and create a personalized treatment plan. There is no obligation, and we offer transparent pricing with flexible payment options.
