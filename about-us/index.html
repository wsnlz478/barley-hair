Our Story

From a small clinic to China's major hair transplant hospital chain

The Barley Journey

Barley Microneedle Hair Transplant Hospital was founded by a team of visionary doctors who recognized the growing demand for advanced hair restoration solutions. What started as a single clinic has grown into China's trusted hair transplant brand with 31 branches across major cities.

Our commitment to innovation led us to develop the patented Microneedle Hair Transplant technique, which has revolutionized the industry by providing higher density, faster recovery, and more natural-looking results than traditional methods.

Today, Barley is recognized as a leader in hair restoration, serving patients from over 50 countries worldwide. Our mission is to help everyone achieve their dream of having a full, healthy head of hair.
