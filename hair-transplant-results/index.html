What to Expect During Your Hair Transplant Journey

Understanding the process helps you feel confident every step of the way. Here's a timeline of what our patients typically experience.

📋

Day 1: Consultation

Free hair analysis, treatment plan design, and discussion of your goals with our expert surgeons.

🏥

Day 2: Procedure Day

The microneedle procedure takes 6-8 hours under local anesthesia. Most patients relax comfortably.

🌱

Month 1-3: Growth Phase

Transplanted hair may shed initially, then new growth begins around month 3. This is completely normal.

✨

Month 6-12: Final Results

Full, natural-looking hair grows in. 95%+ graft survival rate ensures outstanding density and coverage.
